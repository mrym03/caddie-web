Notetaker that speaks and sells.

Caddie is an AI copilot that joins your sales calls. You talk — it researches, recalls, and answers, live, while you're still on the call. No more "let me get back to you."

[ The Problem ]

You're the expert. You still get caught off guard.

Mid-call, a prospect asks the one thing you can't recall — the exact price, the integration, the number from last quarter. You say "let me get back to you." The momentum dies. They don't re-engage.

A notes doc won't save you — you can't read while you talk. A second rep on every call is expensive. And the answer was sitting in your own materials the whole time.

With Caddie on the call, you never stall.

It hears the question, finds the answer in your knowledge base, and feeds it to you — or says it out loud — before the silence. You stay in flow. The prospect gets the answer now, not "later."

Your calls stop leaking. They start closing.

[ What It Does ]

One copilot. Every answer.

01

Answers from your knowledge

Caddie pulls your docs, site, pricing, and emails into a private knowledge base, then answers every question grounded only in your content — no hallucinations, no guessing. It knows your numbers cold and sounds like your best rep.

02

Steps in when you stall

The moment you hesitate — "let me check," "I'm not sure" — Caddie quietly supplies the right fact, leading with "Just to add—." It speaks only when it genuinely adds value. It never pitches, never talks over you.

03

Catches mistakes live

Caddie fact-checks the call in real time. If you misremember a number — or the prospect misunderstands one — it gently corrects it before it sticks, with your knowledge base as the source of truth. Older facts get a "correct me if I'm wrong" check, never stated as gospel.

[ How It Works ]

Live on your calls in three steps.

01

Connect your world

Link Google Calendar, Gmail, and Drive. Caddie learns your product from your docs and your schedule from your calendar — in about two minutes.

02

It joins the call

Caddie auto-joins your meetings straight from your calendar — Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams. No link to paste, no bot to babysit. It's just there when the call starts.

03

It answers, live

As you talk, Caddie listens, retrieves, and answers — out loud or just to you. Say "hey Caddie" to ask it anything mid-call. You stay in the conversation; it handles the recall.
